About Rajapur F.P. School

Rajapur F.P. School is a Government Aided Primary School serving children from Rajapur village and neighbouring communities. We are dedicated to providing a joyful and effective early education with an emphasis on literacy, numeracy, values, and life skills.

Our History

Established to meet local educational needs, the school is a trusted institution that works closely with local families and community leaders to support every child's learning and well-being.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Provide quality primary education developing confidence and foundational skills. Vision: Create a safe, nurturing environment where children grow into thoughtful, responsible individuals.

Infrastructure & Facilities

  • Classrooms equipped with LTM and teaching aids
  • Mid-Day Meal facility for nutritious food
  • Playground for sports and physical activities

Inclusive Learning

  • Library corner with reading materials
  • Safe drinking water and sanitation facilities
  • Community engagement and parent participation

Our Academics

We offer engaging, age-appropriate education from Pre-Primary to Class IV, with a strong focus on foundational skills and active learning methods.

Teaching Approach

  • Activity & play-based learning and group work
  • Daily use of LTM (Learning-Teaching Materials)
  • Focus on storytelling, numeracy, and literacy
  • Regular formative assessments

Co-curricular Activities

  • Drawing, Art & Craft workshops
  • Sports & Physical Education
  • Cultural Programs and Community events
  • Health and Hygiene Awareness

Admissions

Admissions are currently open for all classes (Pre-Primary to Class IV). Please visit the school office during hours or use the contact form to enquire.

Admission Steps

  1. Collect the application form from the school office.
  2. Fill in student and guardian details completely.
  3. Attach necessary documents (see list).
  4. Submit the completed form at the school office.
  5. Admission confirmed after document verification.

Required Documents

  • Original Birth Certificate
  • Student's Aadhaar Card (if available)
  • Guardian's Photo ID and Address Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph of the child
  • Transfer Certificate (for Class II-IV students)
